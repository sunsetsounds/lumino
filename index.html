Portable High Fidelity Audio Reproduction

Design Philosophy

Lumino + is a portable HiFi speaker that places a strong emphasis on sound quality and attention to detail. Before Lumino, high fidelity audio systems were confined to static home setups to provide outstanding listening experience. Portable speakers sacrifice sound quality and attention to detail for the sake of convenience and high unit production in a fast market. Now, Sunset Sounds has developed and engineered Lumino, an audio solution which brings out the best in HiFi and portable speakers by putting superior sound reproduction in a portable form factor. Lumino is also 3D printed to allow for a unique design and handcrafted for the best product finish and care. For the first time, you can bring HiFi quality audio wherever you go!

Audio Architecture

Drivers Built for HiFi

Our pride in Lumino lies in its amazing audio reproduction with a 2-way system. A high excursion 4-inch woofer driving two 4-inch passive radiators delivers deep, dreamy bass never thought possible in a small speaker. Aluminum cones and neodymium magnets ensure excellent linearity and low distortion through the midrange. Finally, a 1-1/8 inch soft dome neodymium tweeter brings detail and clarity unlike any other portable system, complementing the woofer magnificently. This all means that Lumino brings the whole soundstage to you, with big sound (103dB) in a portable package.

Pure and Precise Output

A class-D 2x50W amplifier provides clean, high quality audio to Lumino's drivers. The TPA3116 chip operates at 88% efficiency for better battery life while still having high output levels. Total harmonic distortion and noise (THD+N) is kept in check at 1% with full output, but is optimized for listening around 97db, where THD+N is at a mere 0.06% for the best clarity and detail.


Intelligent Bass Control

An ADAU1701 Digital Signal Processing (DSP) chip is built on-board to allow Lumino to tune the audio system with advanced crossover networks and dynamic bass boost. Lumino intelligently adjusts the bass output based on current listening levels to compensate for psychoacoustic inaccuracies in human hearing.


Connectivity and Power

More than just wireless bluetooth. Lumino features special bluetooth codecs that limit distortion and lower latency in the audio supply chain, supporting APTx, APTx LL, and AAC codecs. In addition, Lumino includes a standard 3.5mm jack so you can stream music from almost any device. As a bonus feature, this 3.5mm jack serves as an output, allowing Lumino to drive other speakers and audio systems when you stream through bluetooth. See our guide here

Lumino + is powered by a 12V 5000mAh li-ion battery pack. This gives our speaker an effective playtime of over 25 hours (varies on volume level). The included AC adapter charges Lumino in about 1.5 hours. This means that you can comfortably use Lumino anywhere to play music for hours on end without having to worry about charging. And when Lumino does need a charge, 30 minutes of charging time is enough for 8 hours of playback time!

Handcrafted and 3D Printed

It might be hard to believe at this price point, but our speakers are handcrafted and assembled with care in San Francisco by our small team. In addition, the chassis is 3D printed in a process that takes well over 72 hours to achieve a design nearly impossible to replicate with other methods. The top half is 3D printed Wood PLA and the bottom half is printed with PETG. Even the flexible handle is 3D printed with a special elastic filament TPU. Our 10+ stage processing and finishing method is highlighted in detail here to show how much attention and care our speakers receive.

Modern Design Language

Lumino strikes inspiration from both traditional and contemporary design. Sleek curves interrupted by bold, sharp edges ensure Lumino has one of the most stunningly unique appearances. RGB LEDs illuminate the logo and the side skirts, lighting up the night scene.
